🔥 Key Findings

  • Salary is the strongest driver of performance
  • Hybrid work model (25–50%) delivers highest productivity
  • Moderate overtime improves performance, excessive OT reduces it
  • Training quantity has no clear impact on performance
  • Promotion does not guarantee higher performance
  • Attrition is not driven by performance

🔍 Business Insights

  • Compensation strategy directly influences workforce productivity
  • Flexible work environments outperform rigid office setups
  • Overworking employees leads to diminishing returns
  • Training programs need redesign focusing on effectiveness

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

  • Increase salary competitiveness to boost performance
  • Adopt hybrid work model for optimal productivity
  • Control overtime to avoid productivity decline
  • Redesign training programs based on quality, not volume
  • Reevaluate promotion criteria beyond performance metrics
